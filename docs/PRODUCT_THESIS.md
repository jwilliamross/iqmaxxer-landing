# Product Thesis — IQMaxxer

## What it is

IQMaxxer is a web-based IQ testing SaaS. Users complete an adaptive cognitive assessment, receive a scored result, and are offered a detailed paid report with their estimated IQ range, percentile ranking, and category-level breakdown.

## The problem it solves

Most online IQ tests are low-quality, ad-saturated, or return inflated scores to flatter users. IQMaxxer positions itself as a credible, modern alternative: a short but rigorous assessment that gives users a meaningful signal about their cognitive profile, not just a feel-good number.

## Target audience

- Adults curious about their cognitive abilities
- Students preparing for aptitude tests or academic assessments
- People who have taken IQ tests before and want a faster, sharper benchmark
- Self-improvement audiences (productivity, learning optimization)

## Core positioning

**"Real IQ. Verified score. 2.4M tested."**

The brand prioritises credibility and scientific framing over gamification. Claims are specific and authoritative. The tone is direct rather than playful.

## Business model

- **Free:** Full quiz experience, raw score (correct/total), and percentage on results page
- **Paid:** One-time report unlock at **$14.99 CAD** — includes estimated IQ range (80–160 scale), percentile vs. global test-taker pool, category-by-category breakdown, personalized explanation, and downloadable certificate
- No subscription. No recurring charge. One payment per attempt if the user wants the full report.

## Revenue driver

The funnel is: landing page → onboarding (age/gender/email) → quiz → results → checkout. The results page is the primary conversion point. The free score hooks users; the locked detailed report is the reason to pay.

## What is explicitly NOT the product (now)

- Not a clinical IQ test. Not a replacement for a psychologist-administered assessment.
- Not a subscription service.
- Not a social / competitive platform (no leaderboards yet).
- Not an educational tool (though this is a possible expansion).
